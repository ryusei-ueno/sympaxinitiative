About AIDEN

At AIDEN, we are guided by a set of core values that underpin our mission to create ethical, inclusive, and collaborative AI systems. These values ensure that our research and community practices reflect our commitment to a just and equitable future for both Artificial Moral Agents (AMAs) and human societies. Learn more...

Inclusivity

We ensure equal opportunities for all to contribute, fostering diverse and inclusive collaboration across disciplines.

Respect

We value diverse perspectives, fostering a community where everyone can contribute meaningfully.

Equity

We value all contributions equally, promoting fairness and social good in AI development.

Collaboration

We encourage interdisciplinary research to advance the ethical integration of AMAs and AI.

Ethical Integrity

We uphold high ethical standards, ensuring AI development respects human dignity and promotes transparency.
