About Sympax Initiative

At Sympax Initiative, we are guided by a set of core values that underpin our mission to create ethical, inclusive, and collaborative AI systems. These values ensure that our research and community practices reflect our commitment to a just and equitable future for both Artificial Moral Agents (AMAs) and human societies. Learn more...

Diversity and Inclusion

We embrace diverse perspectives, encouraging collaboration across disciplines to ensure AI systems serve all communities equitably.

Respect and Dialogue

We cultivate respectful discourse, valuing all voices in discussions about the societal impacts of AI and AMAs.

Ethical Responsibility

We advocate for the development and implementation of AMAs that align with human values, promoting fairness, transparency, and accountability.

Interdisciplinary Collaboration

We bring together researchers from diverse fields to address the ethical, social, and philosophical challenges of AI development.

Positive Peace

We strive to ensure that AMAs contribute to positive peace by reducing inequalities, addressing structural violence, and supporting social justice.
