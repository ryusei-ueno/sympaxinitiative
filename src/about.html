Welcome to the Sympax Initiative!

The Sympax Initiative is a collaborative platform where scholars, researchers, and professionals from diverse fields come together to explore the ethical, social, and philosophical challenges posed by Artificial Moral Agents (AMAs). Our mission is to foster interdisciplinary research and meaningful dialogue, ensuring that AMAs are developed and integrated responsibly into society.


At Sympax Initiative, we emphasize inclusivity, equity, and collaboration. Our goal is to contribute to a future where humans and AMAs coexist harmoniously, guided by principles of ethical integrity, social justice, and positive peace. We strive to create a space where each member actively contributes to a vision of society where AMAs support human flourishing and promote the collective good.


Join us in shaping the future of ethical AI development and fostering positive coexistence between humans and AMAs!

Mission

Our mission is to foster research and dialogue on Artificial Moral Agents (AMAs), focusing on their ethical, social, and philosophical implications. We aim to raise awareness of the challenges and opportunities AMAs present, ensuring that ethical frameworks are developed proactively.

By providing a collaborative space, we work to reduce societal fears and advocate for AI systems that align with human values, promote inclusivity, and contribute to a harmonious future.

Vision

We envision a future where AMAs coexist with humans in positive peace, free from bias and fear. Through interdisciplinary collaboration, we aim to create a society where AMAs support human flourishing, inclusivity, and social equity.

Our vision is a world that values justice, sustainability, and mutual respect, with AMAs contributing to a peaceful, just society.

Scope

The association addresses the ethical, social, and philosophical implications of AMAs. We encourage interdisciplinary research from fields such as philosophy, sociology, ethics, law, and AI to explore AMAs' societal impact. We adapt to emerging issues, focusing on preventing harm and promoting positive societal development in various contexts.

Focus

We focus on ensuring AMAs align with human values and foster positive coexistence with AI. Key areas include ethical decision-making, mitigating bias, and exploring moral agency and accountability. We emphasize positive peace, social justice, and reducing structural violence. Public awareness and interdisciplinary collaboration are key to our efforts.
