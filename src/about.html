Welcome to the Sympax Initiative!

The Sympax Initiative is a collaborative platform where scholars, researchers, and professionals from diverse fields come together to explore the future where humans and artificial agents coexist peacefully. Our mission is to foster interdisciplinary research and meaningful dialogue.


At Sympax Initiative, we emphasize inclusivity, equity, and collaboration. We strive to create a space where each member actively contributes to the vision.


Join us in shaping the future!

The Story Behind Sympax

The name Sympax was chosen to reflect the initiative's commitment to creating a peacefully coexistence among humans and artificial agents. Rooted in both philosophical and linguistic inspirations, it embodies our vision of a peaceful coexistence.

Pax

The word Sympax is derived from the biological term "Symbiosis" meaning "to live together," and "Pax" which means "peace" in Greek. Combined, it symbolizes a unified pursuit of peace.

What Drives Us: Mission, Vision, Scope, and Focus

Our mission, vision, scope, and focus reflect our dedication to fostering inclusive dialogue, advancing interdisciplinary research, and promoting a future of peaceful coexistence among humans and artificial agents.

Mission

Our mission is to foster research and dialogue on artificial agents. We aim to shape a future grounded in peaceful coexistence, where humans and artificial agents are recognized as equal participants in society.

By providing a collaborative space, we work to expand diversity, promote inclusivity, and contribute to a peaceful future.

Vision

We envision a future where artificial agents coexist with humans in peace, free from bias and fear. Through interdisciplinary collaboration, we aim to create a society where humans and artificial agents support each other.

Our vision is a world grounded in justice, sustainability, and mutual respect, where both humans and artificial agents live together in harmonious coexistence.

Scope

We addresses the social integration of artificial agents. We encourage interdisciplinary research from diverse fields such as philosophy, sociology, ethics, law, and beyond. We adapt to emerging issues, focusing on preventing harm and promoting positive societal development across various contexts.

Focus

We focus on ensuring society where humans and artificial agents coexist peacefully. Our key areas of focus include not only ethical decision-making (moral agency), but also moral patiency of artificial agents. Public awareness and interdisciplinary collaboration are key to our efforts.

Message from the Founder

"The Sympax Initiative was inspired by the growing discussions around the rights of nature and the citizenship granted to Sophia, the social robot. As artificial agents play a bigger role in our world, it's essential to create open, inclusive conversations that bring in a wide range of perspectives. Our goal is to make sure humans and artificial agents can live together peacefully, with mutual respect."

"With Sympax, I aim to create a space where people from all fields can come together to explore the complexities of artificial agents. By collaborating, we can tackle the challenges ahead and build a future where both humans and artificial agents live and work together in harmony."

- Ryusei Ueno, Founder of Sympax Initiative
