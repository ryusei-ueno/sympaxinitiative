About Sympax Initiative

The Sympax Initiative is guided by core values that shape our mission: fostering a society where humans and artificial agents can thrive together in peace. These values inform both our research and community practices, reflecting our enduring commitment to building a shared future across human and artificial lives. Learn more...

Peaceful Coexistence

We are committed to cultivating a future where humans and artificial agents can thrive together in peace—beyond domination, fear, or control.

Interdisciplinary Inquiry

We embrace collaboration across disciplines, believing that complex futures require diverse ways of knowing, from science and philosophy to art and technology.

Diversity & Inclusion

We value the presence and perspectives of people from all backgrounds and lived experiences, and we actively work to create spaces that are inclusive, affirming, and just.

Mutual Respect

We uphold the dignity of all beings—human and non-humans—through open dialogue, empathy, and ethical relationships.

Why Join Sympax Initiative?

Join an interdisciplinary network committed to building a society where humans and artificial agents coexist peacefully. Here's why the Sympax Initiative is the ideal platform to support and amplify your research:

Interdisciplinary Collaboration

Researchers from diverse fields can collaborate on groundbreaking work at the intersection of peace studies, artificial intelligence, and post-humanism perspectives. The Sympax Initiative fosters a rich environment for cross-disciplinary inquiry, enabling fresh insights that challenge traditional boundaries and promote holistic understanding.

Cutting-Edge Research in Peaceful Coexistence

By joining the initiative, researchers contribute to pioneering studies on peaceful coexistence between humans and artificial agents. This work explores the ethical and social dynamics of living alongside AI as neighbors, rather than simply as tools—shaping a future where both human and artificial agents coexist with mutual respect and cooperation.

Social Impact and Global Collaborations

Researchers at Sympax have the opportunity to collaborate globally, addressing the social implications of artificial agents in diverse cultural contexts. This initiative emphasizes creating solutions that have a positive and lasting impact on societies worldwide, fostering international collaboration to navigate the challenges and opportunities posed by emerging technologies.
